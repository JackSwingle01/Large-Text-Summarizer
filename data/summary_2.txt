Alice falls down a rabbit hole and enters a strange world where she encounters talking animals and participates in nonsensical activities. She attends a tea party with the Hatter and the March Hare, plays croquet with live hedgehogs and flamingos, and attends a trial where she is called as a witness. Alice eventually wakes up and realizes it was all a dream.The text is about a person who has a simple and loving heart and shares the stories of Wonderland with her children.
